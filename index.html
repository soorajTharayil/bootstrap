Inside the Book...

A quick glance at the book you'll love to know

What happened in Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban Chapter 1?

Harry gets a letter and a birthday gift from his three closest friends: Ron, Hermione, and Hagrid. Ron's family won a drawing and took a vacation to Egypt – he sends Harry a picture of the Weasley clan that appeared in The Daily Prophet.

What happens in chapter 2 of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban?

The TV is on and a news report notifies people that a dangerous convict named Black is on the loose. Harry then hears news that really ruins his day: Aunt Marge is coming to visit. Marge hates Harry and makes his life even more miserable than usual at the Dursleys.

What happens in chapter 3 of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban?

Harry gradually calms down and starts to panic – he just ran away from home after performing accidental, illegal magic. He's convinced that he's going to be expelled from Hogwarts and starts planning his new life as a fugitive outcast.

What happens in chapter 4 of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban?

Harry runs errands and starts buying his supplies for the school year. At the bookstore, he picks up his books for a new subject, Divination, which is about predicting the future. He also learns that the Monster Book he got is a course book for Care of Magical Creatures

What happens in chapter 5 of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban?

Professor Lupin will teach Defense Against the Dark Arts and Hagrid will teach Care of Magical Creatures. Harry, Ron, and Hermione are psyched about Hagrid's new gig. At the end of the feast, they rush up to congratulate Hagrid and then make their way to the Gryffindor rooms along with the rest of their house.
